Enter a hand of cards in the field below

Use the following format (and be sure to only enter 5 cards separated by spaces): {face}{suit}

Where the characters C, D, H, and S represent the suits Clubs, Diamonds, Hearts, and Spades
Card faces are represented by A, K, Q, or J for Ace, King, Queen, or Jack, or simply the number of the card

Example: 6d 3h 10s 2c 6h
